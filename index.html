This is the first group project for the CCSF Coders Club

The Obituary Project is being written by students in the Coders Club at City College of San Francisco.

The Obituary Project

Data has been compile from Obituaries from a single source of the past 30 years. The project main goal is examine the text of each obituary and count the frequency of words and phases to demonstrate how certain words and phrases have come in and out of fashion over time.
