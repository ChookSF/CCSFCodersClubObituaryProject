
Nathan Oliver Boykin
Nathan Oliver Boykin born December 28 1972 died suddenly anuary 15 2004 leaving a void once filled with the buzz and chatter of wideeyed tales the
ceaseless ringing
and chiming of bells and whistles that was his love to those that he knew
This silence echoes a man part child a wiseass part angel a whirling series of contradictions loyal kind and generous  wicked wild and relentless both touching and challenging those in his crossfire He will be missed
