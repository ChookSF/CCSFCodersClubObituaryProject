
Donald Hurst
Dons wife Mary his son Mark and his longtime partner Tan were at his bedside during his final hours He passed into the next life in the early morning
of February 14
Typical of Don he did not depart this earth on Friday the 13th but waited overnight until Valentines Day 
Don was a generous volunteer who helped hundreds of people He served at Project Open Hands Food Bank and for over thirteen years at Coming Home Hospice He was an alcoholic in recovery and after recovering himself helped many men and women to achieve sobri ety
Don was loved by many and will be greatly missed
