
R Bruce Emmett 1943  2004
Bruce passed away on April 30 2004 after a sixmonth battle with lung cancer He was well known in the Castro where he lived for the past 30 years Bruce shared the
last 24 years with his
life partner Gerald Jerry Narberes We will miss those blue eyes His big grin and the rattle of his dice cup
Bruce is survived by his brother Iohn and six sistersinlaw Betty Sally Delores Arlene Lois and Nancy He was preceded in death by brothers Richard Roland Harry Thomas and Donald
A celebration of Bruces life will be held at The Mens Room Bar on 18th St on Sunday May 16th from 2S pm Bruce requested that any donations be made to the AIDS Emergency Fund or to the American Cancer Society His last written words were Love and peace to all
