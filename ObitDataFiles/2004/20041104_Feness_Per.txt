
Per Feness
Per Feness 81 of F535 Oak Ave in San Francisco died on Saturday October 9 after being hit by a car on October 4 Per was born in Bergen Norway but was a longtime San Francisco resident He was associated with La Pentara in North Beach for many years until it closed He then worked for Giovannis in Ghirardelli Square for several years before he retired
There will be two memorial services on of Friday November 5 at the Norwegian Church at the foot of Hyde Street the second at Martunis on Market and Valencia Streets on Wednesday November 10 at 8oclock
He will be missed greatly by his family in Norway and many friends in San Francisco
