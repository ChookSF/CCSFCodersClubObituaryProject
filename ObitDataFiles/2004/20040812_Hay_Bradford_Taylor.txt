Bradford Taylor Ilay 44 of San Francisco California formerly of Houston Texas passed away unexpectedly on August 1 2004 Brad graduated from Texas Tech with a Bachelors degree in Horticulture He went on to work for American Airlines for nearly 20 years He loved softball and landscaping He was known to his family and friends for his love and kindness He will be missed by all Brad was preceded in death by his father Dr Richard C Hay Survived by his mother Martha F Hay his siblings Richard Bill Anne Sandy Holly and numerous nieces nephews and friends The memorial will be held at 1115 am on Saturday September 112004 at The National AIDS Memorial Grove in San Franciscos Golden Gate Park Flight Attendants attending are requested to wear either their regular AA or Hawaiian uniform Soft Ball League Team Members are requested to wear their team uniforms
