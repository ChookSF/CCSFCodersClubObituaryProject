
Kenneth W Wilson  October 27 1949  February 16 2004
After a tough year battle with cancer Kenny died in his sleep early on Presidents Day Born  raised in Springfield Missouri his residence in the Bay Area dates back to the Summer of Love Kenny went through more than a few jobs in retail All American Boy The  Muscle System Whole Foods but would have preferred a life spent weaving rugsor tending garden
A private person he was a very human mixture of softness 8 shell of independence  faithfulness of limited resources 8 flawless taste He will be missed by a widely scattered group of friends from Southern California to Missouri 8 Virginia to Leongatha Australia two brothers Ronald of Florida 8 Robert in Springfield 8 their families his loving companion for the last dozen years Les 8 his dear friend through it all Charlotte
