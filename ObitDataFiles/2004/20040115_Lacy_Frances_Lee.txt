
Frances Lee Lacy
Frances Lee Lacy was born in San Francisco October 2 1929 and passed away December 27 2003 She was preceded in death by her parents Theodore Iames Coulter and Ivy Lucille Grue Coulter She is survived by many loving family members and friends
Fran retired from Retail Clerks Union and then worked part time at Ma/zettis Bakery in Pacifica She was a member of the Christian Science Church in Pacifica an avid supporter of the arts especially donating to the SPCA Legion of Honor all SF museums and the SF Zoo She loved music dancing all animals especially Cocoa and Abby walking by the ocean and spending time with family and friends She continues to live love and laugh
Donations can be made in her memory to the SPCA or the Legion of Honor
