
Bah Tllomas
June 6 1984
San Franciscos Gay community lost a treasured friend this past week when Bob Thomas left us Bob had been an for the past several months with Hodgkins disease
An active member of the current Ambush Softball Team Bob played many years in the Community Softball League as well as the Gay Softball League He had been associated in the past with Twin Peaks Tavern Sutters Mill The Mint and an AllStar with the 1982 World Champion Pendulum Pirates
Words are inadequate at this time as we Send our condolences to his family and many friends Services were handled by the Nephme Society Longtime iriend Skip Anderson requests that those wishing to do so send cash donations in Bobs name to the AIDS Foundation 54 Tenth Street San Francisco CA 94103 I
