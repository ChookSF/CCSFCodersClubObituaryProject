
Philip Ramos Empress Patty
Philip Ramos better known as Patty Packard was taken suddenly by natural causes in San Jose last week Mr Ramos was very active in fundraising for the less fortunate and donated all of the proceeds from his female impersonation performances to charity He was Empress of the Court of Wine and Roses and was very active in the drag community in San Francisco and in his home town of Santa Rosa
He was employed as a psychi
attic technician at Agnew State Hospital In recent months he was active in organizing a South Bay parish of St Priapus Church He is survived by his mother a resident of Santa Rosa Internment will be in the family mausoleum in Santa Rosa
