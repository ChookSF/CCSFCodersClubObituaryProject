
Patrick D Conroy MD
Friends of Patrick D Conroy MD will want to know that Dr Conroy died on Sept 27 after a long illness Dr Conroy was born and raised in Ireland but came to the United States in the 19505 An anaesthesiologist he practiced in Toms River New Jersey and later in Chico Richmond and Oakland California Dr Conroy will be remembered for his sensitivity compassion and wry sense of humor He requested that in lieu of flowers contributions be made to the Intertribal Friendship House at 523 East 14th Street Oakland CA 94606 I
