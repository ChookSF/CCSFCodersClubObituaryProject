
Bluford Jeffery Moor
aka Blue October 6 1965  July 5 2001 Bluford Jeffery
Moor known to his friends as Blue passed from heart failure due to complications of HIV  on July 5th 2001 He will be remembered and celebrated by his mother Gloria
his sisters Karen Mary and Sandy his partner Owen D Edwards his Monday night yoga friends other friends and the Radical Faeries
His contagious smile sparkling eyes and his ethereal way of deeply affecting many peoples lives will never be forgotten He was proud of his art and his contributions to the San Francisco LGBT Pride Celebrations Radical Faerie Freedom Village and the Radical Faerie Film Festival Blue did his best to make the world a better place and short of that at least a lot more fun
