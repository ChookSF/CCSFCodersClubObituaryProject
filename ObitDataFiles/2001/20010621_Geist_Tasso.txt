
Tasso Geist
Tasso passed away June 3 2001 Tasso got up every day with a zest for what life would bring that day Tasso was always willing to share and teach others He
was an activist against social injustice In May of 2001 he received the Robert I Westwood Leadership Award at San Francisco State University for outstanding student organization advisor Tasso is survived by his partner lay and dear friends and family He will be greatly missed
A memorial service will be held at SFSU AIDS Memorial Grove July 24 2001 at 2 pm
Contributions may be made in Tasso Geists memory to Cindy Kolb Fund SFSU 1600 Holloway San Francisco CA 94132
