
William R Lawrence February 22 1940  September 12 2001
William Billy Lawrence died peacefully at home on Wednesday September 12 He was the beloved partner of Mark Shafer He is survived by his sisters Carolyn
Duncan and Lyn dall Sier of La Crosse Wisconsin and his niece Lynne Lintleman also of La Crosse He was born in Wisconsin on February 22 1940 and had lived in San Francisco for the past 20 years Billy and Mark were members of the parish of the Episcopal Church of St John the Evangelist
Billy recently retired from the Bank of America where he was an administrative assistant to an executive vice president
A requiem mass will be held on October 13 at 10 am at the Episcopal Church of St John located on 15th St at Julian in San Francisco
