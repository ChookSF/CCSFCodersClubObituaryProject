
John Derr
John Derr passed on to heaven on July 10 2001 and will be missed by N everyone who knew him John was an English teacher at City college and a member of the Muscle System gym for many years He is survived by his mother Rita sister Terry and her husband David along with their two children John Derr moved to San Francisco in 1985 following his graduation from the University of Wisconsin he was a kind and gentle man who frequently gave to his community We all pray for his safe journey
