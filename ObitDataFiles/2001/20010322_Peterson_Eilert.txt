
Eilert Peterson
Eilert Peterson died on March 5th of complications from AIDS Born on September 2l 1956 in Gillette Wyoming He moved to San Francisco in 1984 working at Chevron before retiring He developed a large and supportive group of friends volunteered at Under One Roof and was active in several groups His mother two sisters two brothers several nieces and nephews survive him
A Celebration of Eilerts life was held Sunday March 11th  at one of his many favorite places in San Francisco Fort Point under the Golden Gate Bridge We all will miss him
Friends plan a permanent memorial at the AIDS Memorial Grove in Golden Gate Park Contact Allen Constant at 720 8848
