
Steven A Lessure
Steven died peacefully and suddenly on September 212001 in San Francisco He was born September 26 1948 in Ohio He was a graduate of Ohio State University and served in the US Navy after college He was honorably discharged from the Navy in 1971 and moved to San Francisco Steve was an early participant in the Shanti Project working there for several years Relatives in Cincinnati Ohio survive him
