	
David I Rose June 7 1946  April 26 2001
Dave Rose died suddenly from a massive heart attack on April 26 2001 David was born and raised in Detroit Michigan but moved to San Francisco
shortly after college He was employed by City College as a database manager He was buried on April 29 2001 next to his lover Bruce H Greenberg who died in 1987 He is survived by many loving friends in San Francisco and by relatives in California and Michigan
