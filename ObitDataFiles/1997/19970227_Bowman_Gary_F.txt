
Gary F Bowman Sept 15 1944  Jan 30 1997
Gary died after a long battle with AIDS He will be
 remembered by his family and friends for his great sense of humor and the dignity with which he handled his illness He is survived by his mother Erika and his sister Carol Boersema There was a memorial service at the Most Holy Redeemer Church on February 4
Garys ashes were strewn over San Francisco Bay as he had requested He will always be near the city he loved so much
Gary will be greatly missed by all  who knew him He was a kind and loving soul
