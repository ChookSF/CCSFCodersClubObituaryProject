
Douglas Walsh Nov 2 1947  Dec 2 1996
Doug found eternal peace on December 2 at Laguna Honda Hospice The cause of death W35 cerebral lymphoma Dougs brother Gary will return his ashes to New Brunswick and there he will rest beside his parents Many will remember Doug from his work at the Huntington Hotel the Press Club and other dining rooms in San Francisco He will be greatly missed by his friends Bill Peter Helen and Ion Dougs beloved cat Marilyn found a new home at Laguna Honda Hospice Many thanks to the wonderful people there who gave Doug such loving care Thanks also to Project Open Hand and the AIDS Emergency Fund for all their help
