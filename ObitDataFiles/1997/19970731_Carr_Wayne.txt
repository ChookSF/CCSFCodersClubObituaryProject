
Wayne Carr Jan 15 1934  July 2 1997
Wayne Carr RN was a psychiatric nurse Whu frequently visited San Francisco He will be missed by his Bay Area friends Howard Paul Jack Robbie Ron and Brian A native Bostonian he died there after a long and courageous battle with cancer An exceptional cook Waynes other passions were gardening and the theatre He will be missed by his cats Torti and Mandi and by his partners of 27 years Roger Brighthill
