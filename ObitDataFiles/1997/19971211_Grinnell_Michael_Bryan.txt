
Michael Bryan Grinnell July 10 1958  Nov 22 1997
Bryan died suddenly after a long battle with AIDS on November 22 He was a resident of San Francisco for over 20 years According to Bryans last wishes there will be a memorial service held in  Scarsdalc NY and a funeral in Bern Switzerland
Bryan is survived by his mother Margrit father Roy sister Lisette brother Tim beloved friends Graham Chrissy Lori Kay Manuela Dibz and Sally as well as many friends he made during his many years in San Francisco The Grinnell family would like to thank Dr Poscer his many friends at the Infusion Center of Mt Zion Medical Center and the many services that gave Bryan so much loving support during his illness
Bryans gentle loving nature and love for life will be forever remembered and sorely missed by us all
