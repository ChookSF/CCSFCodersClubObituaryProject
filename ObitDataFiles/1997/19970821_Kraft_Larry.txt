
Larry Kraft August 7 1997
Larry Kraft died peacefully at Most Holy Redeemer Coming Home Hospice in San Francisco on August 7 at 9 am With him at the end were his wife Betsy brother Joe and best friend Richard
We want to thank everyone for their kindness and support We will miss Larrys bravery and his wonderful sense of humor After a ii Catholic Mass his ashes will be scattered on his favorite California mountain top His spirit remains with us forever Donations may be made in Larry Krafts name to Coming Home Hospice 1 I5 Diamond St San Francisco CA 941 I4
