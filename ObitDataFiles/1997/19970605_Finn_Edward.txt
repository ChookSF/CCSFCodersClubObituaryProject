
Edward Finn May 5 1997
Edward Finn 39 longtime resident of San Francisco and Provincetown died peacefully on May 5 of testicular cancer at his home on the Cape He was surrounded by his sister Barbara brother Billy and several close friends including Angus Mc Caslin
Ed was first diagnosed with cancer six years ago The disease was in remission for four years due to his doctors in SF and Boston as well as his unfailingly positive outlook but resurfaced last summer Edward was one of those rare individuals who had a profound influence on everyone he met He was a warm generous erudite man with an inimitable style His sharp wit entertained
his friends and coworkers that umque sense of humor was one of Eds
greatest gifts Well read wellspoken and welltraveled Ed had a lust for life that few could match He will be missed and remembered by all of his many friends
