
Peter Tatone 1928  1997
Peter Tatone died on April 27 at Kaiser HospitallSl of a stroke He was a native San Franciscan who lived most of his life in the Excelsior District later moving to the Geary and Van Ness area He belonged to many organizations including Gay Men Over 40 Gay and Lesbian Outreach to Elders and the Golden Gate Connection He worked for the city and county of San Francisco for 25 years
Peters most enduring virtue was his ability to attract and make friends Among them were Paul G Bob 1 Don K Art H loc Romeo H Ed 5 Perry W and Marvin Peter was warm and friendly and above all a good listener to peoples joys and woes He will be sorely missed by his extended gay family and by the slot machines in Reno and Las Vegas
