
Reginald Tom Tidy 1915  1998
Born in Hornscy London England His early music studies were interrupted by WW and it was while serving in the British Army in Spain that he met sweet Molly who would be his wife for 40 years Togethery they lived in British Honduras then Montreal and finally in Corte Madera Tom/Reg was music librarian at the College of Marin and well known as an usher at Davies Symphony Hall and the War Memorial Opera House He had a particular love for the ballet and supported the San Francisco Ballet as well as many other companies and offered continuing encouragement to young dancers and singers
ln recent years Tom Reg lived at The Carlisle where he made many new friends and sponsored music recitals for the residents
A memorial will be held at The Carlisle on Saturday September 19 at 2 pm
