
Harold Parker
Harold Parker passed away October 17 1998 Beloved brother of Irving Lillian adoring uncle of Iocl Fran A truly wonderful man who will be missed not only by his family but by all the friends he made while working at the Opera and Ballet He was a teacher in the San Francisco Unified School District for 30 years at Balboa High School and the School of Business and Commerce He was former owner of the Ritch Street Baths in San Francisco during the 1970s
Funeral scrviccs were held at Mount Sinai Mortuary in Los Angeles on October 23 1998
