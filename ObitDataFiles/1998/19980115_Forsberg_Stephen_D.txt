
Stephen D Forsberg January 12 1998
Stephen D Forsberg a native of Worchester Mass died January 12 at age 54 Steve had lived in San Francisco since 1972 He was an active supporter of Coming Home Hospice first as an employee and later as a volunteer He was also recently a lay chaplain at San Francisco General Hospital A funeral mass will be held in Steves memory on Saturday January 17 at 11 am at Most Holy Redeemer Church I00 Diamond Street  in San Francisco in lieu of flowers Sieve Requested donations be made to the restoration fund for Most Holy Redeemer
