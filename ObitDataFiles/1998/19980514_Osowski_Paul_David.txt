
Paul David Osowski August 17 1947  April 29 1998
 Not farewell but faring The gentlest sweetest most compassionate loving man we were privileged to share our lives with became an angel on April
29 succumbing to liver cancer after 18 years of successfully living with HIV/AIDS Paul entered this life in Chicago and left it in Sea Ranch inspiring everyone with his honesty strength courage sensitivity humor caring and love Paul taught us how to live to the fullest and how to die with dignity and grace
A Berkeley graduate Paul lived in San Francisco for many years when not traveling the world always sharing his special way of seeing that world He was on the Advisory Board of The Community Consortium and worked with HIV Health Services Planning Council and ACT UP
Paul is reunited with his mother Helene and survived by his loving sister/best friend Pamela father Aloysius siblings Alan Joyce Monica Denice and Mark their families and innumerable friends doctors nurses and helpers who became his family An August memorial is planned
