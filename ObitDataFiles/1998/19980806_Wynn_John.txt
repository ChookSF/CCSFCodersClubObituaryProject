
John Wynn Feb 24 1958 Feb 27 1998
John died at home with his devoted brother Bill by his side in his beloved San Francisco John is survived by a large extended family and his long time nurse and social worker John was a loving and creative person who wrote poetry and could make art out of buttons scraps of material or anything available He had a marvelous sense of humor and was a very courageous and independent person though at times overly generous He was unique and precious and will be deeply missed
