
Michael Paul Hudson March 21 1948  Sept 14 1998
Michael or as he was known by his friends Mikl was a resident of the Bay Area for more than 20 years Mild was an employee of the Orpheum Golden Gate and Curran theatres for 19 years however the theatre was only onc of Mills many loves Mikls kind heart and generous free spirit will be missed by all that knew him Most of all we will miss the laughter and originality that his presence provided us
God speed my friend you are sorely missed
Services for Mild will be held at the Metropolitan Community Church at 150 Eureka Street in San Francisco on October 17 1998 at l pm A reception and auction to benefit Family Link will follow at the Edge Bar 4149 18th St
