
Roger E Roeck April 25 1998
Rogcr E Rocck died at his home in Sebastopol on 25 April at the age of 52 of kidney failure and other complications from AIDS He is survived by his partner David Meeker and many family members Rog a resident of Sonoma County for 12 years was very involved in many fundraising events for AIDS charities He also gave of his many talents to the Sebastopol Area Chamber of Commerce of which he was an active member He had a BS degree in Hotel and Restaurant Management from the Univarsity of Denver and worked in professional food service for 30 years He is greatly missed by many people who were fortunate to have known this generous man
