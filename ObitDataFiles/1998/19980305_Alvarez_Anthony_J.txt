
Anthony J Alvarez Nov 7 1963  Feb 14 1998
On Valentines Day morning Tony passed away in his parents arms at their home in Sunnyvale He will be deeply missed by his loving and devoted family as well as those friends and loved ones whose lives he touched
Tony was a natural salesman full of charm and wit We will always remember his dazzling smile gentle heart and loving nature Our lives are richer and warmer for having known him
