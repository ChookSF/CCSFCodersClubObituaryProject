
Howard Young
Howard passed to the other side in the
early morning of Nov 12 Growing up in Mount Vernon NY he moved to California in 1965 to attend UCLA In the early 19705 Howard moved to northern California where he spent a year writing music in Guerne ville Settling in San Francisco in 1973 he operated several plant stores Since 1980 he and his partner Jack Garcia have been in the chocolate business at Church and Market streets
With him were his sisters Claudia Michalson and Linda Shappero of NY his cousin Marilyn Corwin of LA and his lover Greg Standish
The family asks that contributions be made in Howards behalf to the Howard Young Memorial Fund American Foundation for AIDS Research 1515 Broadway NYC 10036 V
