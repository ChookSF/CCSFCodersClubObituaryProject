
Jon Jay Merriman
Jay left this life peacefully five minutes after midnight April 16 at Coming Home Hospice holding the hands of his friends Kathryn and Gordon He was 43
Born in Philadelphia he lived most of his life in Houston and San Francisco A quiet and private person he was active in the Monday and Wednesday night bowling leagues and represented Hawaii in the first Cay Games As a travel agent and earlier working for United and Continental Airlines Jay enjoyed extensive travels throughout the world
His positive attitude was an inspiration to those who knew him and he will be deeply missed He is survived by his brother Dorian Johnston and sister Barbara
