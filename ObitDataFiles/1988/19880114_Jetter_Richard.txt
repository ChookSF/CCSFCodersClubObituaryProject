
Richard Jetter
Richard Jetter died of AIDS related conditions on Dec 26 1987 at Mt Zion Hospital in San Francisco He was 34 His mother and father and friends were grateful to be with him in his final hours
Richard was a talented composer who studied at the Peabody Institute and at Cal Poly He enjoyed a year of teaching music in the backlands of Liberia as a Peace Corps volunteer Richard was a whiz as a computer programmer and teacher
Richard sought a spiritual path through life and hoped for one through death A memorial service and internment will take place on J an 23 at Forest Lawn Hollywood Hills Los Angeles
