       
Jonathan James
Artist Jonathan James passed away on Nov 3 at the Coming Home Hospice With him at the timewere his mother
and many of his friends
He had recently exhibited a retrospective of his drawings and prints at AAA Studios San Francisco
An enthusiast of public transport particularly the historic trolleys he produced an art exhibit entitled Trolley Art in 1978 He was also a longtime employee of the law firm of Jackson Tufts Cole and Black
There will be a celebration party in his honor on Saturday Dec 3 at 8 pm at AAA Studio 233 14th St San Francisco
