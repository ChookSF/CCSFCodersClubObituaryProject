
Richard Haas Jr
Richard Haas Jr died in East Hartford Connecticut July 13 1988 at age 50 He was born and
raised in Harlford Connecticut He came to San Francisco in 1976 and is best known to many friends for his generous and helpful nature
Most of his
career was in the
collection field Dick owned and maintained a boat at Pier 39
Dick is survived by his mother Mary Haas brothers Phil and Jerry Haas of East Hartford and sister Rosemary Miller of Manchester Connecticut
