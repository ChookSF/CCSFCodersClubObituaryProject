
David R  Hall
David R Hall 34 departed this life peacefully on Oct 16 1988 two years after being diagnosed with AIDS
Born and raised in Daytona Beach Florida David came to San Francisco in 1979 He was manager of the Polk Street branch of American Savings and Loan Known for his love of celebrations David will be missed by his many friends
David is survived by his lover of ten years Tony ChambersHall his parents Barbara and Kenneth Hall his sister Barbara Ann Fogelman his brother Hay Hall and his nieces and nephew
It is requested that donations be made to the AIDS Emergency Fund Y
