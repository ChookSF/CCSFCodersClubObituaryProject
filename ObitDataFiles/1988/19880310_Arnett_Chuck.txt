Chuck Arnett wellknown artist and longtime San Franciscan died peacefully at 1245 prn March 2
A Louisiana native and former New York theater dancer Chuck came to San Francisco in the early 19605 and quickly made an impact on the bar scene with his famous murals at The Tool Box I964 and then at The Stud 1963 He continued his bar associations with The NoName The Red Sm and The Ambush His work in recent years has been less public
His extraordinary talent and forceful personality left lasting memories to all who were fortunate enough to know him
