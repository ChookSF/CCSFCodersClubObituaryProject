
Robert Jorgenson
Robert Jorgenson passed away in Petaluma on Sept 13 1988 He is survived by his mother Dorothy Paris and sisters Jeanette Ferraro of Santa Cruz and Patricia McCandless of Red Bluff He is also survived by numerousaunts uncles and cousins
Jorgenson was a native of Petaluma and died at age 36 A memorial service was held Sept 16 in Petaluma
Donations in his memory may be made to the Hospice of Petaluma 415 A St Petaluma the American Cancer Society So Co Unit 826 St Helena Ave Santa Rosa or the Sonoma County AIDS Foundation 3325 Chanate Rd Santa Rosa U
