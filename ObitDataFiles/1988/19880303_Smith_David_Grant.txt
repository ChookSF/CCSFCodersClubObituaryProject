David Grant Smith died of AIDS Feb 24 He was a concert musician a photographer a teacher a brother a friend and above all a decent human being
David was an onery fella with little patience for attitude substance abuse insincerity or dogma He cherished honesty clarity and perserverance He also loved photographing men and his work was frequently published in Inches Bear Honcho and Mandate
