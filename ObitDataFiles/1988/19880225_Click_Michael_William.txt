
Father Michael William Click passed away at 330 am Monday Feb 22 of complications due to AIDS With him at home were his lover Scott his sister Janice his parents and several close friends
A priest of the Episcopal Chunch Michael was devoted to God to the ministry of healing and Watked for the cause of peace His love of music and his appreciation of beauty in all of its forms enriched his ministry and the lives of all whom he touched We who love him are thankful for the gift of his life
A memorial service will be held at The Episcopal Church of St John the Evangelist 1661 15th St at 2 pm Sunday Feb 28 In lieu of flowers please send any gifts or donations to the AIDS Ministry of the Episcopal Diocese of California I
