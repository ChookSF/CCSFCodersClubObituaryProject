
Frederick Eugene Welch March 20 1943June 28 1983
Born and educated in Boston Fred adopted San Francisco as his home Many years in hotel management the last ten
years as manager of the Palo Alto Hotel he was a longtime active member of the Polk Street Merchants Association
Fred a member of Grace Cathedral was a quiet man whose personal actions reflected his deep belief in God and the dignity of the human spirit On special holidays Fred was noted for giving members of his Palo Alto family that special touch which made it not just a hotel but a home
