
Rick DuB0is
Rick died peacefully at Santa Rosa Community Hospital on Sept 10 1988
Rick DuB0is was a native of Colorado moving to San Francisco in 1981 He attended Colora
do University and graduated with a Bachelor of Science degree in Journalism from Ohio University He then joined the US Navy and served as a Public Affairs Officer attaining the rank of Lieutenant After the Navy he attended Virginia Theological Seminary and was ordained an Episcopal Priest He did volunteer work at St Dorothys Camp Bishops Ranch in Sonoma County and the Sonoma County AIDS network
He is survived by his father his sister Marilyn his two sons Kittrick Randall and Jay Wescott and his daughter Jennifer Lee He was a kind and loving man
