
Wm K Billy Horton May 15 1955March 31 1933
Born and raised in the HaightAshbury and a third generation San Franciscan Billy was a graduate of Riordan High and SF State He was also coowner of Nedas Flowers a longtime member of the HaightAshbury Improvement Association and St Agnus Parish
He died peacefully with his mother and father at his side and is survived by his parents James and Marie his seven brothers Bob Jim David Larry Allen John and Joe and hislover Bob l Holloway all of whom shared in his love of life He has left behind three special friends Neda Richard and Jack and many other warm and loving members of the San Francisco community We will all greatly miss him
Memorial services were held last Tuesday at St Agnus Parish and Gifts in his name may be made to the Shanti Project
