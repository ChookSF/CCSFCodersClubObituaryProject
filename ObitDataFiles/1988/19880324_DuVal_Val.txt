
Val DuVal Nov 10 1918March 13 1988
A legend left this world for a better one when Val DuVal passed away after a brief illness
Val was a dress designer enterminer and active participant and supporter of many community activities and functions His motto was The more outrageous the better
He will be missed by his many friends and neighbors in the Duboce Triangle where he had a shop for 12 years and by his partner of 15 years Jerry Elsinger who will try to keep the shop open with community support U
