
Thomas Robert Long
Thom Long born in Kokomo Indiana in 1951 grew up in the South living in Florida and in
Selma Alabama during the civil rights movement Adulthood took him to big cities New Orleans Chicago and finally his beloved San Francisco Well respected in the telecommuni
cations field his career took him elsewhere many times but he could never stay away from home for long
