
Renato Aquino de Leon
Renato Aquino de Leon MD died Saturday July 2 1988 of complications of AIDS He
is survived by his lover of six years Ron Green his parents Juanita and Primitive de Leon and his brothers Antonio and Romulo de Leon
Dr debeon was a psychiatrist who worked until his illness for the Center for Special Problems where he was admired and loved by his colleagues and friends
