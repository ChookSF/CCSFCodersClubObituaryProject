
Eric Allen Guitar Guthrie
Born June 9 1961 he passed from this life April 21 1988
Orginally from Pennsylvania Eric spent his adult life in the Bay Area finally settling in the city he most loved San Francisco
Eric was a printer by trade and was last employed at Postal Instant Press on Polk and Post streets
Eric leaves us with fond memories of his outrageous guitar sessions his passion for the Beatles his beautiful posters and most of all his wonderful hugs
Eric will be greatly missed by Nick Dick Pierre Kurt and Chuck
Goodbye buddy we love you
Erics ashes will be scattered outside the Golden Gate at 3 pm Wednesday May 18 U
