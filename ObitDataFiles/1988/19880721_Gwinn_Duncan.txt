
Duncan Gwinn
Duncan Cwinn died at Garden Sullivan Hospital on July 15 after a long struggle with AIDS
Born in Cornwall England in 1940 Duncan came to San Francisco in 1978 First employed as a stamp dealer by Stanley Gibbons he later became the proprietor of News on 24 a newspaper store in Noe Valley Duncan was also a longtime active member of Dignity and a friend of Golden Gate MCC He is survived by his friends Hosea and Kim Turner and by numerous brothers sisters nieces and nephews in London England
