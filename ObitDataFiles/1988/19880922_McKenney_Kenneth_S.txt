
Kenneth S McKenney April 14 1947Sept 1 1988
Kenneth born in Durham North Carolina passed away in Dowling Michigan Sept 1 He was a member of the US
Navy from 1966 to 1970 when he left with an honorable discharge He was also a Greyhound bus driver in San Francisco for 13 years
Kenneth is survived by his mother Pauline
brother Ronald sister Karen Rochelle and many friends throughout the US A memorial service was held Sept 3 at Battle Creek Michigan Cremation has taken place with his ashes to be scattered at Lands End at a later date
