
Lincoln Kauahi
Lincoln Kanahi one of the most beloved and respected gentlemen in our community passed peacefully to the next life on Sunday Ianuary 27 at Kaiser Hospital following a brief illness San Francisco was home to Lincoln for most of his adult life and for many 1 years he was the popular manager at Polk Streets famed New Belle Saloon where his customers and friends literally numbered in the thousands Lincoln is survived by family members in Hawaii and many many loving friends Our dear Lincolns remains have been returned to Hawaii for services
Rest in peace Mr Lincoln  we will miss you very very much
