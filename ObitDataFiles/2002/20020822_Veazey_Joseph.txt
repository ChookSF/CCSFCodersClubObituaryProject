
Joseph Veazey
Joseph Veazey born April 23 1960 was called to the final roundup May 8 2002 at San Francisco General Hospital A man who loved cowboy boots he was a Texan who never made it to the White House Although he had few ties to family or friends those who did know Ioseph knew he had a kind and honorable soul Described as a selfmade man and a man of few words yet he had a great sense of humor He played pool like a pro and we like to picture him now sinking 3cushion shots in a billiards parlor somewhere in that Great Beyond He will be remembered by his friends and staff at San Francisco General Hospital and Laguna Honda Hospital Donations in his name may be made to Project Open Hand 730 Polk St San Francisco CA 94109 phone 415 4472326
