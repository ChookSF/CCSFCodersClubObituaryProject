
Deric Scott Marshall
Deric Scott Marshall passed away on Wednesday December 1 1 2002 at San Francisco General Hospital Our dear friend Deric a longtime resident of San Francisco studied and loved music avidly His talent in the music field earned Deric the 1987 BMIGrammy Award for Classical Composing
The past five years Derie worked as a bartender at the bar known as My Place and will be fondly remembered by his many regular patrons and forever celebrated by his closeknit family of coworkers Deric is missed dearly by his mother Derrelyn stepsister Melissa step brother Wally and his loving partner Joe
With his big smile and even bigger heart Derics sweet memory remains
