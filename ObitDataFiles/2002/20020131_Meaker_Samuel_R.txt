
Samuel R Meaker September 23 1925 January 14 2002
Samuel R Meaker died on Monday January  14 2002 at the age of 76 after many years of emphysema and military service related illness He received
his Bachelor of Arts
degree in History and Master of Arts degree in Geography in 1950 and 1951 respectively at Boston University He made his home in San Francisco and was a travel agent and half owner of Mark Tours and Travel
Inc in San Francisco from 1978 to 1989
Following his retirement he became active in working and volunteering for gay service organizations including Operation Concern Shanti and American Association of Physicians for Human Rights AAPHR Kind intelligent and elegant in his manner Sam lived an arresting life and was a faithful friend to the people and animals in his life
