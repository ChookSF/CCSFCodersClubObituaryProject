
Michael Kantor December 30 1999
Michael Kantor died in his sleep on December 30 1999 at the age of 42 Michael was a regional sales manager for an electronics firm He was born
in Pennsylvania
and moved to the Bay Area 21 years ago and lived in the Sacramento area the last several years Michael was known and beloved by his many friends and colleagues He was always full of life and with his bright blue eyes and infectious laugh always lit up any room he entered His parents john and Connie his brothers Steve Joe Tom and his sisters Teri Marie and Patty survive Michael Mike loved to work play and party hard and will always be remembered for being the life of any party We will always love you Michael  David Gerard Robert Chris and Tony
