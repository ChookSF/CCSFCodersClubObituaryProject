
Matthew H Rayburn
A retired stock brokerage clerk Matthew H Reyburn 74 died of respiratory failure at Southwest Washington Medical Center Wednesday May 31 2000
Mr Reyburn was born on October 4 1925 in Kansas City Missouri and lived in Vancouver the last 10 years
He is survived by one sister Ruth Foster of Independence Missouri and his special companion Donald Pollock of Vancouver
Mr Reyburn enjoyed collecting cut glass and attending auctions
There will be no service His body was cremated HamiltonMylan Funeral Home is in charge of arrangements
Memorial contributions may be made to the American Diabetes Assn 3434 Martin Way NE Olympia WA 98506
