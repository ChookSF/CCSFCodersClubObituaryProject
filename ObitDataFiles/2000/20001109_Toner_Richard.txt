
Richard Toner July 9 1953 October 10 2000

Strauss He was a very warm friendly and dependable man that lived life to the fullest Richard is survived by his adopted father and soul mate Lee Toner and his longtime friends Frank Malinaro and tom OMalley He will be very much missed Rest in peace my friend
