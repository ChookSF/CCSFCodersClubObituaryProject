
Robert Daniel Jr December 25 1965 October 19 2000
Robert made his transition among the fall foliage in Maryland At the time of his passing from AIDS he was surrounded by his partner mother and two sisters He calmly and peaceful Robert loved cooking music and nature and was very committed to his community He was hysterically funny and loved life He worked for AIDS Project of the East Bay for five years helping others struggling with the disease He loved creating a loving home for him and his partner Bill He charmingly never outgrew the childhood excitement of Christmas season He also loved hosting an annual pumpkin carving party
 He leaves behind his partner Bill Flanigan and dog Nicki mother Grace Daniel father Bob Daniel Sr sisters Lavita Daniel Chris Clark and Rebecca Reid and six nieces and nephews
We wish you lots of light and unconditional love as you continue your journey Well be along soon
