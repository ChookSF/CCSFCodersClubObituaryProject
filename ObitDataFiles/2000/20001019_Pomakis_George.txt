
George Pomakis April 10 1951  October 2 2000
George a resident of Oakland originally from Lynn Massachusetts passed away on October 2 from a short illness He is survived by his father Michael Pomakis of Florida and a sister in Ipswich Massachusetts
George was the owner of Elements on Sacramento St in San Francisco He enjoyed being at home with his two dogs Maya and Yanni and took pride in his garden He helped neighbors and friends develop their own garden oases Georges generosity was felt by his friends neighbors and business associates
A gathering will be held at his home at 486 43rd St in Oakland on October 21 from 25 pm for those who would like to celebrate his life For more information or directions please contact Stephen Massey at 510 6527011
