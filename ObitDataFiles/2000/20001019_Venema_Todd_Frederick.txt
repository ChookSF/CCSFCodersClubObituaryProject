
Todd Frederick Venema
Todd passed away on September 22 2000 at his home in Laytonville California with his loving partner of three years David Aldridge at his side along with his four wonderful pcts Cosmo Simone Max and Knightly after along and heroic battle with HIV/AIDS Todd was born in Seattle Washing ton on September 21 1958 to Walter and Anita Venema He is survived by four brothers two sisters and a host of nieces and nephews Todd is also survived by a host of wonderful friends who will miss his warmth laughter and appreciation for life
There will be a memorial held at his home on October 21 2000 beginning at 12 pm
