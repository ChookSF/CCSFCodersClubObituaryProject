
Arthur J McDonald
Arthur I McDonald 51 of San Francisco died Sunday February 27 2000 from an apparent heart attack while driving his motorcycle Art had been employed as a piano player for various restaurants and bars in the San Francisco area He recently volunteered as a piano player at the Diamond Senior Citizens Center in San Francisco Art is survived by his two sisters Robert Lou Schnabel of Jacksonville Florida and Sally M Hite of Newburgh Indiana his stepmother Melva McDonald of Chatham Virginia and nieces and nephew He was preceded in death by his parents Edna and Robert McDonald and his sister Sandra Lynn Massa
Memorial services will be held at a later date in Chatham Virginia The family requests friends to share their memories about Art and mail them in c/o Sally Hite 10666 Williamsburg Dr Newburgh Indiana 47630 Memorial contributions may be made to Diamond Senior Citizens Center in San Francisco
