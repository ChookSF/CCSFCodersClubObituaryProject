
Tighe J Foley January 31 1956  August 8 2000
Tighe I Foley a longtime resident of San Francisco California died of  AIDSrelated complications He had been the Executive Director of Ayurveda Health  Education Resources of San Francisco He was also the Executive Director of Positive Resources a nonprofit agency that helps place HIVpositive persons on jobs
Tighe was born in Portsmouth Virginia and raised in New Hampshire Many friends family and his cats Rodan and Paris survive him
A celebration of his life will be held Saturday September 23 2000 at the San Francisco Church of Religious Science 280 Claremont Blvd at 2 pm In lieu of flowers contributions can be made in Tighes honor to the AIDS Memorial Grove or the San Francisco Zen Center
