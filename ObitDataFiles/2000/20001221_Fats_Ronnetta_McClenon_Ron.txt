
Ron McClenon AKA Ronnetta Fats November 22 2000
Ron McClenon AKA Ronnetta Fats Cowgirl II of All California passed away at his home in Clearlake California after a long battle with AIDS
He leaves his sister and brotherinlaw Audrey Garcia and Frank Garcia and his partner of 15 years Thomas Dunn and his two Boston Terriers Tiffany and Beethoven and many friends
He was always a star and hopes his star will always shine He will be missed by all who knew him May he find happiness on his long trip home
