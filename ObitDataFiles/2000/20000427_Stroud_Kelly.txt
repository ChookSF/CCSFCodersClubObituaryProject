
Kelly Stroud October 11 1957
March 28 2000
Our beloved Kelly was taken from us on March 28th He died peacefully and comfortably at home where he wanted to be in the company of his birds and his friends Ben and David He is survived by his sister Debbie his brother Danny his niece Nicole and nephew Michael
There was no other with a bigger heart and a kinder soul than Kelly He will reinain in our hearts forever and be missed by all who knew him   There are no memorial services planned However it is suggested that donations be made in his name to the San Francisco Department of Animal Care and Control on Harrison Street
