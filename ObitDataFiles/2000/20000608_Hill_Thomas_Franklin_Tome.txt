
Thomas Franklin Hill
Thomas Franklin Hill a former resident of Mendocino died in San Francisco on May 272000 at the age of 67 Tome moved to San Francisco in 1994 after he retired from the Bank of America Tom worked for many years in the Mendocino branch of the Bank of America He was a deacon in the Mendocino Presbyterian Church and was well known for his many kindnesses After moving to San Francisco he became a member of the Metropolitan Community Church He is survived by his sister Betty Carney of Covington
Ohio nieces and nephews and friends His partner Vernon Johnston died in 1982 A memorial service will be held at 4 pm on Iune I8 2000 at Metropolitan Community Church 150 Eureka St San Francisco Memorials may be made to the Hanna Boys Center Sonoma California
