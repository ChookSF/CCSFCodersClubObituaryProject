
Hal S Heller
Hal passed away peacefully on the evening of October 10 from complications of pneumonia His sister Renee lover Johnny and other close friends were at his side Having been a part time instructor be went on to become the owner of Bay Area Motorcycle Training And after having been a student of the Renaissance Business school he became one of its board members The leather community were also beneficiaries of his skills and sensibilities in his teaching others the importance of respect and safety in play He will always be remembered for his generosity love of adventure and compassion His outstretched hand open cars and comforting voice will be missed by all Memorial services will be held Sunday October 31 at a location to be announced
