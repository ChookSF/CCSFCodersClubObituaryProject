
Brian Corr
Brian lames Corr 45 died peacefully in his sleep in early November He enjoyed gardening in his small plot in the neighborhood garden on Dolores at St and was especially proud of his roses Brian had a degree in interior design and enjoyed refinishing furniture He was active in the Stop AIDS Project over the past several years
Brian was also a founding member of the first perhaps only Bipolar Support Group for gays and lesbians with mood disorders which met weekly for over three years Brian was a great friend and always a gentle quiet support for someone in need An informal gathering of friends and simple memorial will be held at his home on Sunday December 12 For more information call Ken at 415 9288160
