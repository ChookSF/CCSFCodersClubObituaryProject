
Chester Duane Lauritzen
19341999
Chester D Lauritzen died suddenly on Wednesday July 7 of heart failure Chester grew up in Portland Oregon but lived here in the city he
loved for nearly 40 years Chester was a manager in retail shoes the last 25 of which was for Byrons Shoes in San Mateo
He is survived by his sisterinlaw Lena and two nephews and a niece John Peter and Ann Lauritzen of Olympia Washington and also by his close extended family which has been honored to call him a friend
A celebration of his life and times will be held at the Giraffe Lounge Polk and Sutter Streets this Saturday August 7 from 24pm
