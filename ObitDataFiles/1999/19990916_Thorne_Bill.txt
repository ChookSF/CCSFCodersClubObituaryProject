	
Memorial Saturday for ACT UP/G G member
a memorial service will be held this Saturday September 18 for Bill Thorne a longtime AIDS activist and ACT UP/Golden Gate member Thorne died August 4 of liver failure He was 35
ACT UP/Golden Gate member Jeff Getty said the group will present Thornes family members
with a plaque at the service which starts at 2 pm at Metropolitan Community Church San Francisco 150 Eureka Street In addition to his activism Thorne was a contributor to the Bay Area Reporter as a member of the ACT UP/Golden Gate Writers Pool
