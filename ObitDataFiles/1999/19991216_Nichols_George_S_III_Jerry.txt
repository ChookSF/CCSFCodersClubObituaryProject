
George S Nichols Ill
 George S Nichols III Jerry died at home in San Francisco on November 19 1999 He was born to George S Nichols and Lillian Brown Nichols on March 16 1924 in South Pasadena California His sisters were Iean Nichols Steinhart and Nancy Nichols Stephens His halfsister and halfbrother are Michelle Nichols Matson and Phillip Spalding Nichols He was also the uncle of the late David Edward Stephens He will be interred at Alta Mesa Memorial Park in Palo Alto A private memorial gathering is being planned
