
Michael Vachon January 5 1965  December 27 1998 
The Gypsy Waif is no longer with us He passed away on December 27 1998 from complications of AIDS Survivors include his partner of five years Mike Handley and his beloved pugs Pepsi and
Napoleon
He was born in Maine and moved to San Francisco in the late 1980s and lived there until 1993 Then he moved to Murfreesboro Tennessee where he met his partner Mike IIandley and took on the role of HIV/AIDS Coordinator for the American Red Cross in Murfreesboro where he touched many lives and helped people in need
 People will remember him as a kind and gentle loving person willing to help others Michaels remains will be brought to San Francisco at a later date to be scattered into the wind to be a free spirit oncc again
