
Russell Carl Driscoll
Russell Carl Driscoll 42 a resident of San Francisco for 15 years died Monday February 22 1999 after a long illness He was the beloved son of Fay Whitaker and Frederick Driscoll of Fort Myers Florida He is also survived by two brothers Ronald Driscoll who is serving in the US Navy in Japan and Richard Driscoll of West Warwick Rhodc Island He leaves four nephews and many uncles aunts cousins and friends
A memorial service was held in San Francisco on February 27 1999 Contributions may be made in Russells memory to Project Open Hand or the San Francisco AIDS Foundation
