
Miguel Rosner
Miguel Rosner a ubiquitous spirit in San Francisco for over 20 years died in New York on September 25 following a long struggle with eosinophilia myalgia syndrome As a member of Bay Area Gay Liberation and an editor for Magnus Miguel contributed to the blossoming of the gay Left here in the 1970s He was the perfect New York/San Francisco hybrid an Edward Carpenter socialist with a discursive knowledge of world affairs a passion for opera and film noir and a fondness for caf afternoons Wellread and proud of his Sephardic heritage Miguel had acquired one of the most extensive and beautiful collections of Iudaica in private hands His sisters Bella and Mimi survive him Anyone interested in coming together to remember Miguel should please call Denny Smith at 2521312
