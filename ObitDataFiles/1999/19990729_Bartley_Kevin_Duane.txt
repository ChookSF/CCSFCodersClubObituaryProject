
Kevin Duane Bartley
Kevin Duane Bartley of Denver Colorado made his transition on Friday July 23 1999 Kevin who grew up in the Jefferson/Perry Indiana area had made his home in San Francisco where he
managed the Galleon Bar 8 Restaurant Later he worked for American Business Communication as production manager He was active in the community and was Prince Imperial IV of the Emperors Council of San Francisco
After retirement Kevin returned to Denver where he volunteered at the Colorado AIDS Project and Project Angel Heart He is survived by his parents five sisters a brother his friend and partner Ierry Coletti his beloved dog Hannah his dear friends Brenda and Don Whiteman and many others whose lives he touched He will live in our hearts forever
