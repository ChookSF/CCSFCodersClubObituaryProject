
Laramie J Simpson January 29 1999
Laramie Jim Simpson aged 27 of Murray Kentucky left this world suddenly on January 20 1999 A graduate of Phillips Academy and Georgetown Uni
versity Laramie
was fluent in Spanish Russian French and Chinese and loved nothing more than helping people less fortunate than himself In San Francisco for six months in 1998 he touched on the lives of the many people he met through his jobs at the Muscle System on Hayes St and at Dolores Street Community Center The many friends he leaves behind will never forget his intelligence and sensitivity boyish charm and ready laugh He is survived by his mother Bettye Bennett sisters and partner Tony Akers all of Kentucky A memorial service for Laramie will take place at 3 pm on Sunday February 7
