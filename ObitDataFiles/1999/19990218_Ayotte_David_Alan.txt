
David Alan Ayotte August 20 1948  January 30 1999
David Ayotte expired at San Francisco Veterans Administration Medical Center at 845 am on January 30 1999 after a brief hospitalization for multiple medical problems With him during his
illness were his longtime good friends
David Cowley and Ben Franklin Davids legacy includes a book of poems Echoes From Within Wit com passion and a strong love of travel and the sea characterized David He is survived by his brother Norman and three sisters Charlotte Lynn and Sandi
Private memorial services were conducted Memorial contributions in the memory of David may be made to the charity of your choice
